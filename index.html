Best time to visite Egypt

Winter

Between December till February is the best time to visit Egypt, weather is warm and the Sun is shining, specially the upper of Egypt, like Luxor, Aswan and Dahab, where you enjoy the History of the acient Egyptian, and see all the Historic Temples

Spring

Between the end of February till the end of May, is the period where you can enjoy sun without being burn, its the suitable time to go to beach and enjoy the sun, its the Red sea period, places like Safaga, Hurghada, Sharm Elsheik where you can enjoy the clear sea water and water game like diving, snorkling and boat trips

Summer

Between the begining of June and the end of september, its the period where you can enjoy the Mediteranean Sea. and here you have to visit Alexandra, the queen of the Mediteranean Sea, where you will enjoy the wonderful weather and best Fish taste.

Automn

Between the end of september till the end of November, its the period where you can enjoy the Red Sea again, with the hot Sun but never burn, and enjoy the mountain safari in the desert and riding the camels till the Santi Katerin Mountain.
