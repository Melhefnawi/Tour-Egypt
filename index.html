Places to visit in Egypt

  • Hurghada

    Hurghada is one of the main capitals of snorkeling and scuba diving, due to the pristine clear blue waters of the Red Sea and abundant marine life including beautiful coral reefs.

  • Cairo

    The city holds one of the Seven Wonders of the World the Great Pyramid of King Khufu, the world’s oldest and biggest statue the Sphinx and the house of mummification the Valley temple.

  • luxor

    Luxor means “The Palaces” and “The city of Hundred Doors“. It is considered an open-air museum for the fact that on an area on 417 sq km (161 sq mi) the Valley of the king, the Karnak temple, Queen Hatshepsut temple

  • Alexandria

    Alexandria is a Mediterranean port city in Egypt, that has the Lighthouse, ranking among the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World as well as the infamous Alexandrian library.

Best times to visit Egypt

Winter

The period between December and February is the best time to visit Egypt. The weather is warm and the sun is shining, especially in the southern part of Egypt, like in Luxor, Aswan and Dahab. There you can enjoy the ancient history and see all the Historic Temples.

Spring

Between the end of February and the end of May, is the period where you can enjoy the sun without getting burnt. It's the perfect weather to go to beach and enjoy the sun. This period is also called 'The Red sea period'. The most visited places are Safaga, Hurghada, Sharm Elsheik, where you can enjoy the clear sea water and activities like diving, snorkeling and boat trips.

Summer

Between the beginning of June and the end of September is the period where you can enjoy the Mediterranean Sea. You can visit Alexandria, the Queen of the Mediterranean Sea, where you can try some of the world's best fish dishes.

Autumn

Between the end of September to the end of November you can enjoy the Red Sea again, where the sun shines and warms without burning. You can experience Mount Sinai safari, desert road trips and riding the camels up the Santi Katerin Mountains.
